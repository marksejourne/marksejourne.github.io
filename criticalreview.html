The Critical Review
I redesigned and worked with a team of other students to develop a new website for Brown's Critical Review, a course review publication which was established in 1976. I used Illustrator, Figma, and Sketch to execute the site's art direction / branding. The site's development incorporated Node.js, Express, MySQL, Passport.js, EJS, D3, HTML, CSS, and Shibboleth.
