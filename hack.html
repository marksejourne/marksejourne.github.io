Hack@Brown 2018

Hack@Brown is Brown University’s student-run Hackathon that draws over 500 students from all over the world to the Providence, RI campus every year.

Overview

I collaborated with a team of 6 other designers and the development team to build a website and design posters, stickers, apparel, merchandise, etc. for Brown’s student-run hackathon. The website incorporates a sleek landing page, registration flow, various modals, and a hacker dashboard (for accepted hackers) to process 2,500+ applicants from 120+ schools, both domestic and international.

All phases of design took place within a 5-week timeline. We began this process by developing a design language and branding aesthetic to communicate hack@brown’s approachable and inclusive identity, then carried that branding through our initial sketches, wireframes, prototypes, mockups, etc, and into the finalized website.
